# Index: Programmatic Accreditation Standards — 2022 Amended Edition
## National Authority for Quality Assurance and Accreditation of Education (NAQAAE)

---

## Overview

This document represents the Programmatic Accreditation Standards issued by NAQAAE in Egypt, 2022 Amended Edition. It applies to external review reports for educational programs at faculties and universities. The amended edition includes **detailed guidance notes** for each indicator.

## Key Themes of Programmatic Accreditation

1. **Program Mission and Management** — Mission, leadership, marketing, partnerships
2. **Program Design** — Academic standards, program structure, specifications, matrices
3. **Teaching, Learning and Assessment** — Teaching methods, field training, assessment diversity, fairness
4. **Students and Graduates** — Academic support, activities, graduate follow-up
5. **Faculty** — Numbers, competencies, selection, development, research
6. **Resources** — Financial, physical, health & safety, technology
7. **Quality Assurance** — Feedback, reports, self-evaluation, improvement
