# Standard (7): Quality Assurance and Program Evaluation — 5 Indicators (Amended)
## Programmatic Accreditation Standards — NAQAAE 2022 Amended Edition

---

### Indicator 7-1: Feedback from Students and Faculty
Periodic feedback exists from students and faculty to measure their satisfaction with the program and the educational process. It is analysed and used for continuous improvement.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Surveying students' opinions on courses at the end of each semester
- Measuring and evaluating student satisfaction with the effectiveness of the educational program annually (admission and transfer policy / academic advising / student support / student activities / field training / treatment style / distance learning / physical resources / etc.)
- Measuring and evaluating the satisfaction of faculty and teaching assistants periodically regarding the educational process and the working environment
- Identifying the means used for measurement and the participation rates from each category relative to the total
- Identifying the results of feedback analysis and the means by which they are discussed with the relevant stakeholders/councils
- Evidence of how the feedback has been used over at least the past three years

---

### Indicator 7-2: Feedback from Graduates and Employers
Periodic feedback exists from graduates and employers on the program's appropriateness in meeting labour market requirements. It is used to modify and update the program.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Surveying graduates' opinions on the program and the appropriateness of its knowledge and skills in preparing them for the labour market, along with their suggestions for improvement
- Surveying employers' views on graduates' level and professional readiness, along with their suggestions for improvement
- The appropriateness of the measurement means used and the numbers participating from each category
- Identifying the results of feedback analysis and the means by which they are discussed with the relevant stakeholders/councils
- Evidence of how the feedback has been used over at least the past three years

---

### Indicator 7-3: Course Reports
Periodic reports exist for the courses that demonstrate adherence to the published course specification. They include analysis of examination results and pass rates and their implications, analysis of student feedback results, and improvement and development plans.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Availability of annual course reports for at least the last three years
- Quality and completeness of reports for all courses, containing the necessary data and appropriate improvement plans specifically concerning the development of course content, teaching methods, the examinations, the physical support facilities for the educational process, or other aspects — taking into account student examination results and analysis of student questionnaires

---

### Indicator 7-4: Annual Program Reports
Annual reports exist for the program that include: verification of compliance with the program specification, measurement of students' acquisition of the knowledge, skills and competencies defined in the program, and annual enhancement and development plans with the participation of relevant stakeholders.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Availability of annual program reports for at least the last three years
- Quality of annual program reports and their inclusion of the necessary data and information
- The appropriateness of annual improvement plans in terms of precisely identifying areas for development and enhancement and the appropriate measures, and their alignment with the improvement plans in the course reports for the same year

---

### Indicator 7-5: Monitoring Enhancement and Improvement
The outcomes of the program's enhancement and improvement process are discussed and monitored, and the ways of benefiting from the self-evaluation process are identified on a periodic basis.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Discussion of the program's self-evaluation results with relevant stakeholders and in the governing councils, identifying appropriate corrective measures and having them approved by the formal councils
- Identifying the rates of implementation of the corrective measures in previous annual improvement plans over the past three years

---

> **Note:** The original 2022 edition includes 6 indicators, additionally covering:
> - Original 7-1: A system exists for managing and ensuring the internal quality of the program, affiliated with the institution's quality assurance unit. It includes: objective indicators for measuring educational effectiveness, means for collecting data and information from relevant objective sources and methods for analysing them, and ways of using them to improve the quality and competitiveness of the program and improve the educational environment
