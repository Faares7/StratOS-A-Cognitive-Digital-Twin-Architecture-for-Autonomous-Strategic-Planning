# Standard (6): Resources, Learning Sources and Support Facilities — 4 Indicators (Amended)
## Programmatic Accreditation Standards — NAQAAE 2022 Amended Edition

---

### Indicator 6-1: Financial Resources
The program has sufficient and diverse financial resources to achieve its mission and objectives in accordance with the nature of its activities and student numbers.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Identifying the adequacy of expenditure in the program generally, as evidenced by the appropriateness of human resources, physical resources and support facilities for the educational process relative to educational activities and the number of enrolled students

---

### Indicator 6-2: Premises and Support Facilities
The program has appropriate premises and support facilities suited to the nature of the program, the applied teaching and learning activities, and student numbers, in line with the Authority's reference specifications.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Identifying the adequacy of the teaching rooms used specifically by the program and the appropriateness of their equipment for the educational process and student numbers
- Identifying the adequacy of training spaces (laboratories and equivalent) and the technical support facilities specific to the program, and the appropriateness of their equipment for the nature of the courses and student numbers
- Identifying the adequacy of educational resources such as up-to-date books and references, electronic sources specific to the program, etc.

> **Note:** The original edition also includes under this indicator: buildings and facilities (lecture halls, classrooms, workshops, laboratories, clinics, farms, offices, etc.) and equipment and installations appropriate to the nature of the program; accessibility facilities for students and staff with disabilities; and a healthy environment (lighting, ventilation, cleanliness).

---

### Indicator 6-3: Health, Safety and Occupational Security
The requirements and equipment for security, health and occupational safety are available in a manner appropriate to the nature of the program and students' needs, and precautionary procedures are applied in response to emergency circumstances.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Identifying the adequacy and effectiveness of security and safety means (directional signage, fire extinguishers and training in their use, etc.)
- Identifying the practices for maintaining occupational safety, preventing infection, and the availability of appropriate equipment (such as laboratory and workshop safety, handling hazardous materials and infectious diseases, etc.)
- Crisis and disaster management procedures (an evacuation plan and training / dealing with COVID-19 and similar events / etc.)

---

### Indicator 6-4: Digital and Technological Infrastructure
The program has an appropriate and sufficient digital (technical/technological) infrastructure for its needs and for the numbers of faculty and students.

**Guidance Notes:**
- Identifying the electronic programmes and applications used in the program's field of specialisation
- Identifying the support facilities specific to the electronic infrastructure for distance/online learning (e.g. server — preparation of asynchronous learning content — e-learning platform — virtual classroom applications for synchronous learning — virtual laboratory programmes — technical and IT support)
- The extent to which the digital infrastructure is actually used (examples of students and faculty using it in the program's educational activities)

---

> **Note:** The original 2022 edition includes 7 indicators, additionally covering:
> - Original 6-5: The program has digital systems/Management Information Systems (MIS) and integrated databases that are updated periodically, enabling the secure storage, retrieval and exchange of documents
> - Original 6-6: The program has access to a well-equipped library with diverse, appropriate and sufficient resources for the nature of the program and the needs of students and faculty — including up-to-date books and references, specialised electronic sources and global databases — which are used by both faculty and students
> - Original 6-7: The classrooms, laboratories, equipment, installations, support facilities, utilities, physical infrastructure and electronic infrastructure used by the program are maintained and updated on a regular and continuous basis
